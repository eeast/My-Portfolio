About Me

Hello! My name is Eric Easthope. I am starting my journey into development, and this page is meant to showcase some of the work I have done thus far. My background is in accounting, with most of my programming knowledge coming from UT Austin's Web Development Bootcamp and a handful of college CS courses covering the fundamentals of computer science, data structures, algorithm design and analysis, and a course in advanced programming concepts. I've made a collection of webpages, shown below, as well as a variety of offline applications, ranging from traditional problem solving algorithms to server hosting, HTTP request and response, and even mobile application development. Please take a look at my GitHub page, and reach out to me with any questions! Thank you for visiting!
